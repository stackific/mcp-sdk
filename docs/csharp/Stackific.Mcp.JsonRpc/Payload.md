## Remarks

The TypeScript SDK expresses these shapes as Zod schemas whose `safeParse(...).success` signals validity. The idiomatic C# analogue used here is a family of `Is…` predicates over JsonNode, plus a [`ParseEmptyResult`](./Payload/ParseEmptyResult.md) that reproduces the non-passthrough stripping behaviour of `EmptyResultSchema`.
