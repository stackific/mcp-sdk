## Remarks

The AS SHOULD cache documents (R-23.12-k) and SHOULD apply CIMD security considerations such as a trust policy over allowed client-hosting domains (R-23.12-l). An optional `trustHost` predicate rejects documents on disallowed domains before they are stored, and a `no-store`/`no-cache` directive (or a non-positive `max-age`) keeps a document out of the cache.
